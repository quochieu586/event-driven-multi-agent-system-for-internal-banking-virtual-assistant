# FINANCE & TREASURY OPERATIONS GUIDE
Version: 2026.A
Department: Global Finance Control

## 1. Corporate Credit Line (CCL)
- SME LIMIT: Maximum $250,000. Requires 2 years of audited financial statements.
- ENTERPRISE LIMIT: Up to $5,000,000. Requires Board approval and asset collateralization.
- INTEREST RATES: Calculated as SOFR (Secured Overnight Financing Rate) + 2.5% margin.

## 2. Employee Expense Reimbursement
### Travel Policy
- Flight Class: Economy for flights under 6 hours; Business for transcontinental.
- Per Diem: $75/day for domestic; $120/day for international (excludes lodging).
- Submission: All receipts must be uploaded to the 'Oracle-Finance' portal within 30 days of the expense.

## 3. Tax & Audit Compliance
- VAT Filing: Quarterly on the 15th of the month following the period end.
- Audit Protocol: Any transaction over $50,000 triggers an automatic 'Level 2' compliance review by the Senior Controller.
