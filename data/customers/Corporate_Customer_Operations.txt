# CORPORATE BANKING PROTOCOLS
Department: Corporate & Institutional Support

## 1. Corporate Customer Segments
- SME (Small/Medium Enterprise): Annual turnover up to $10M. 
- MME (Mid-Market Enterprise): Annual turnover $10M - $100M.
- GLOBAL: Annual turnover exceeding $100M.

## 2. Treasury Management Services
Our corporate department provides "Auto-Sweep" functionality for MME and Global clients. This automatically transfers excess funds from checking accounts into high-yield overnight investment vehicles at 5:00 PM EST daily.

## 3. Onboarding Requirements for Corporate Entities
To open a corporate account, the expert agent must collect:
1. Certificate of Incorporation.
2. Authorized Signatory List (ASL) signed by the Board Secretary.
3. Beneficial Ownership Disclosure (identifying any individual with >25% stake).
4. NAICS industry classification code for risk assessment.
