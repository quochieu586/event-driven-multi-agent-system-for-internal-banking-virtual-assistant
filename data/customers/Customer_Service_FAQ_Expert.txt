# INTERNAL FAQ: CUSTOMER SERVICE EXPERTS

Q: Can a retail customer open a corporate account?
A: No. They must be referred to the Business Development Team. Retail accounts are for individuals; corporate accounts require legal entity documentation.

Q: What is the 'Cooling-Off' period for new personal loans?
A: All retail personal loans have a 14-day cooling-off period during which the customer can cancel the loan without penalty, provided the principal is returned.

Q: How do we handle 'High Risk' corporate jurisdictions?
A: Any business operating in a country on the 'Gray List' must undergo Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) and be approved by the Compliance Director before a corporate account is finalized.
